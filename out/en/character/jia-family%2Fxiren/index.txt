<h3>1. 判词（第五回 · 程乙本）</h3>
<blockquote>
<p>画着一簇鲜花，一床破席，也有几句言词，写道是：</p>
<p><strong>枉自温柔和顺，</strong>
<strong>空云似桂如兰。</strong>
<strong>堪羡优伶有福，</strong>
<strong>谁知公子无缘。</strong></p>
</blockquote>
<p><strong>[现代白话译注]</strong> 枉自温柔和顺，空有似桂如兰的品性。可羡那优伶（蒋玉菡）有福，谁知与公子（宝玉）无缘。</p>
<p><strong>[Hawkes 译文]</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>In vain was her gentleness and compliance,
Empty the praise of fragrance like cassia and orchid.
Lucky the actor who is blessed in his lot,
But the young master is fated to have no part in her.
<em>Hawkes, Vol. 1, p. 201</em></p>
</blockquote>
